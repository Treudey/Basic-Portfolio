About Me

Ever since I printed my first “Hello World!”, I've gotten the coding bug and have dove in head first, immersed myself in the programming world. Every day I am trying to learn as much as I can about web development improve my skill set.

I used to be an actor and I bring along my love of interacting with people and enjoy working in an environment with a strong focus on teamwork. I focus on building and maintaining strong relationships with my co-workers in whatever job I work at. I love to make my colleagues and employers happy by working extremely diligently and producing efficient and reliable work.
